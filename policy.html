Policies

📦 Shipping Policy

Order Processing: Orders are processed within 24-48 hours of placement (excluding weekends and holidays).

Standard Shipping: 7-15 business days. Estimated delivery times are guidelines only.

Express Shipping: 3-7 business days at additional cost where available.

Free Shipping: Available on all orders over $29 USD. Applied automatically at checkout.

Order Tracking: A tracking number will be emailed within 48 hours of your order shipping.

International Shipping: We ship to most countries worldwide. Import duties and taxes are the responsibility of the buyer.

PO Boxes & APO/FPO: Delivery to PO Boxes and military addresses may take additional time.

↩️ Return Policy

30-Day Return Window: You have 30 calendar days from the date of delivery to initiate a return.

Eligibility Requirements:

  • Items must be unused and in original condition
  • All original packaging, tags, and accessories must be included
  • Proof of purchase is required

Non-Returnable Items:

  • Intimate apparel and swimwear (hygiene reasons)
  • Perishable goods and food products
  • Custom-made or personalized items
  • Gift cards and downloadable products

How to Return: Contact our Customer Service team to obtain a Return Authorization (RA) number. Items returned without an RA number may be refused.

💰 Refund Policy

Full Refunds: Issued when items are received damaged, defective, incorrect, or not as described.

Partial Refunds: May apply for items returned after 15 days, items without original packaging, or items with signs of use.

Refund Method: Refunds are credited to your original payment method only.

Processing Time: 5-10 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item.

Shipping Costs: Return shipping is free for defective, damaged, or incorrect items. For change-of-mind returns, the buyer is responsible for return shipping costs.

💳 Payment Policy

Accepted Methods: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.

Security: All payment transactions are protected with 256-bit SSL encryption and PCI-DSS compliance.

Currency: All prices are listed and charged in US Dollars (USD). Currency conversion fees may apply for international cards.

Payment Timing: Your card is charged at the time of order placement.

Failed Payments: If payment fails, we'll notify you to update your payment method. Orders with unresolved payment issues will be cancelled after 3 days.
