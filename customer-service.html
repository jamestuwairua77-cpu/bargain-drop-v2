Frequently Asked Questions

📦 How do I track my order? +
Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also check your order status in the My Orders section.
↩️ What is your return policy? +
We offer a 30-day return window from the date of delivery. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Check our Return Policy for full details.
🚚 How long does shipping take? +
Standard: 7-15 business days. Express: 3-7 business days. Free standard shipping on orders over $29.
💳 What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.
🔄 Can I cancel or change my order? +
Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement. After that, please contact support and we'll do our best to help.
