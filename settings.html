↩️ Return Policy

+

30-Day Return Window: You have 30 calendar days from the date of delivery to initiate a return.

Eligibility: Items must be unused, in original packaging with all tags attached.

Non-Returnable Items: Intimate apparel, perishable goods, and custom-made items.

Return Process: Contact customer service to obtain a Return Authorization (RA) number.

Refund Processing: Refunds are issued within 5-10 business days after we receive the return.

💰 Refund Policy

+

Full Refunds: Issued when items arrive damaged, defective, or incorrect.

Partial Refunds: May apply for items returned after 15 days or without original packaging.

Refund Method: Refunds are credited to your original payment method.

Processing Time: 5-10 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item.

Shipping Costs: Return shipping is free for defective/damaged items. Buyer pays return shipping for change-of-mind returns.
